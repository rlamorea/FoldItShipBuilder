Select/Proto Technology Track Min. TL Description
/ 7 The ability to travel through normal space faster than the speed of light (C+). A Star Trek-style Warp drive is a prime example of this.
/ 7 The ability to jump a starship from one location to another in an instant after some delay while the mechanism activates. A Battlestar Galactica-style jump drive is an example of this.
/ 7 The ability to push a starship into another realm of space where rules of distance or physics allow for rapid travel between points in normal space. A starship’s normal propulsion systems move the ship through this otherspace. Star Wars-style hyperdrive or Babylon 5-style hyperspace jump points are examples of this.
/ 5 A network of “gates” that allow a starship to jump between them (as described by the Jump track) when activated.
/ 6 A network of “gates” that allow a starship access into and out of otherspace (as described by the Otherspace track) when activated.
/ 7 A form of reactor that allows for super-efficient power generation using exotic fuels (like antimatter or pixie dust).
/ 7 Ability to artificially manipulate gravity.
/ 6 A method to convert power directly into thrust, removing the need for the propulsion systems to consume fuel.
/ 7 Ability to mask a starship from detection by all sensors (including visual) through some means.
/ 6 Ability to use energy to redirect an incoming attack.
/ 6 Ability to use energy to absorb the damage of an incoming attack.
/ 7 Combines both deflection and absorption shielding.
/ 7 Ability to create objects from raw elements.
